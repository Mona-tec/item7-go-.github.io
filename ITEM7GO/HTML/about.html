Bringing the True Taste of Nigeria Straight to You

Welcome to Item7Go, your number one stop for delicious, freshly prepared Nigerian meals. We are more than just a restaurant — we are a celebration of authentic African flavors, warm hospitality, and the joy of good food shared with family and friends. At Item7Go, we believe that food is not just something you eat — it’s an experience, a story, and a feeling of home. Every dish we serve carries a piece of Nigeria’s rich culture, cooked with passion, care, and a deep respect for tradition.

Our Story

Item7Go was born from a simple idea — to make tasty, home-style Nigerian meals easily accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. We noticed how busy life can get, and how hard it is to find real, satisfying meals that remind you of home. So, we created a place where great food meets convenience. From humble beginnings, our goal has always been the same: to serve happiness through every plate. Whether you’re craving smoky jollof rice, rich egusi soup, or tender grilled chicken, we make sure every bite delivers that authentic taste of home.

Our Food Philosophy

We don’t just cook — we create experiences. Every meal at Item7Go is prepared fresh daily by talented chefs who understand what true Nigerian food means. We use only high-quality ingredients, sourced responsibly, and blend traditional recipes with a modern touch to bring out unforgettable flavors.
Our kitchen is built on three values:

✅ Freshness: We prepare every meal as if we’re cooking for family.

✅ Authenticity: Our recipes are inspired by Nigerian roots, cooked with the same love you’d find in any home kitchen.

✅ Speed: Whether dine-in, pickup, or delivery — your food comes hot, fresh, and fast.

What We Offer

✅ Classic Nigerian Dishes: Jollof Rice, Fried Rice, Moi Moi, Egusi, Ogbono, and more.

✅ Delicious Proteins: Grilled Chicken, Turkey, Fish, Beef, and Suya.

✅ Convenient Options: Eat in, pick up, or get your meal delivered right to your door.

✅ Affordable Prices: Great food shouldn’t break the bank — and at Item7Go, it never does.

Our Mission

To bring authentic Nigerian food to everyone with speed, quality, and love. We want every customer to feel the comfort of home through our meals — whether you’re on your lunch break, hosting an event, or enjoying dinner with friends.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s most trusted name in local fast dining — known for flavor, freshness, and customer satisfaction.
